About Us

Despite the critical importance of vaccinations in preventing diseases, there is a widespread issue of missed or delayed vaccine doses due to poor tracking and communication. Patients often lack awareness of their vaccination schedules, while hospitals and doctors struggle to manage and monitor immunization records efficiently. The problem is further compounded by the lack of a unified system connecting patients, doctors, and hospitals for streamlined vaccine management. Vaccine vault eliminates the need to rely on paper work which is generally used in hospitals as records. This system is a vaccine management platform enabling clients, doctors, and hospitals to track and update vaccination records. This system notifies users of missed vaccines and provides revised schedules, creating a seamless way to manage and monitor vaccination progress.
